## What We Offer

**Trauma-Informed Therapy** — Compassionate, evidence-based care that prioritizes safety, trust, and empowerment.

**Brainspotting (Phases I & II Trained)** — A powerful, neuroscience-informed therapy that helps process deeply held trauma through brain-body connection.

**Works with Families** — Improving communication and relationships through a systemic, inclusive approach.

**Intensive Sessions** — Extended appointments (3 to 8 hours) for clients seeking deeper, focused therapeutic work.

## Experiential Therapy

In partnership with [Pathtaker](https://pathtaker.org), a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, we provide outdoor therapeutic experiences like Backcountry Backpacking. These nature-based sessions offer immersive healing and personal growth in the wilderness.

## Holistic Wellness Collective

We are proud members of the Seeds of Change Therapy Collective — a collaborative network of wellness professionals including:

- Licensed Mental Health Therapists
- Yoga Instructors
- Massage Therapists

Together, we provide holistic, integrated care that nurtures both mind and body.
